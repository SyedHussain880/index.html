NOVA Coffee – Logo Design

NOVA is a clean and modern coffee logo designed with a minimal cup illustration and steam forming the word “NOVA.” The concept blends typography with the coffee cup shape to create a simple but memorable identity for coffee brands, cafés, and roasteries. The smooth curves and balanced structure give the logo a professional appearance.

The warm bronze tone adds an elegant feel and works well on dark backgrounds, making it suitable for packaging, coffee cups, shop signage, menus, and digital branding.

This logo is designed to create a strong and recognizable coffee brand identity while keeping the design simple, elegant, and versatile.

Logo Features

  • Unique steam typography concept
  • Minimal coffee cup icon design
  • Clean and modern branding style
  • Premium bronze color aesthetic
  • High-quality vector design
  • Works on dark and light backgrounds
