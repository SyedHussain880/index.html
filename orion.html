Orion Construction — Brand Identity

The Orion logo represents strength, structure, and progress in the construction industry. The circular symbol with horizontal layers reflects architectural floors and the step-by-step process of building development.

The circle also suggests a rising horizon, symbolizing growth and forward movement. Paired with a bold modern sans-serif wordmark, the identity feels reliable and contemporary.

The color palette uses construction orange to represent energy and safety, balanced with deep black.

Logo Features

  • Construction-inspired geometric symbol
  • Horizontal layers representing building structure
  • Circle symbolizing unity and stability
  • Rising horizon concept representing growth
  • Modern bold typography
  • Scalable design for signage and digital use
