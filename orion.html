Orion Construction — Brand Identity

The Orion logo represents strength, structure, and progress in the construction industry. The circular symbol with horizontal layers reflects architectural floors and the step-by-step process of building development. It visually communicates how structures are built layer by layer.

The circle also suggests a rising horizon, symbolizing growth, ambition, and forward movement. Paired with a bold modern sans-serif wordmark, the identity feels reliable, contemporary, and scalable across both digital and physical environments.

The color palette uses construction orange to represent energy and safety, balanced with deep black to convey strength and professionalism.

Logo Features

  • Construction-inspired geometric symbol
  • Horizontal layers representing building structure
  • Circle symbolizing unity and stability
  • Rising horizon concept representing growth
  • Modern bold typography
  • Scalable design for signage, vehicles, and digital use
