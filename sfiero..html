SFIERO-software house

SFIERO is a modern logo designed for a software and technology company. The design combines clean geometric typography with a dynamic circular wave symbol that represents innovation, digital flow, and continuous technological growth. The blue gradient palette reflects trust, reliability, and intelligence, which are essential qualities for a modern tech brand. The minimal and balanced structure allows the logo to work effectively across digital platforms, applications, and corporate environments.

Logo Features

  • Modern geometric typography
  • Circular wave symbol representing technology and data flow
  • Blue gradient palette for a professional tech feel
  • Clean and minimal corporate style
  • Scalable for apps, websites, and branding
