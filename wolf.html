WOLF — Esports Logo Design

This project explores a bold and modern esports logo built around the name "WOLF" The goal was to create a strong visual identity where typography and illustration merge into a single symbol.

The concept integrates wolf elements directly into the letterforms. The tail flows from the first letter, the central characters maintain a solid competitive structure, and the final letter transforms into the wolf's head. This approach creates a seamless connection between the animal and the team name, giving the logo a powerful and aggressive personality suitable for esports branding.

The design process began with a rough pencil sketch to explore proportions, flow, and shape balance. After refining the structure, the sketch was digitally traced and cleaned in vector format to achieve a sharp, scalable logo suitable for professional use.

Logo Features

  • Bold esports-style typography
  • Wolf elements integrated into the letterforms
  • Dynamic tail flowing from the first letter
  • Wolf head forming the final character
  • Clean vector design for scalability
  • Strong silhouette for easy recognition
